We have begun construction of the data base by consulting local experts in various localities, collaborating with colleagues elsewhere who have worked with similar data (a shoutout to our friends at mappingthegayguides.org!), and extracting data from LGBTQ+ travel guides published from the 1960s through the mid-2010s. We recognize that the accuracy of this sort of information, from these sorts of sources, will always be both uncertain and contested. For this reason we try to "ground truth" as much of the data as possible (using a variety of methods) and have included an indication for each piece of data as to how accurate we believe it to be. Your contributions to this process will help immensely with that process!
For reference, the data associated with the table is explained below:

vsid Unique identifier for a venue at a specific year
vid Unique identifier for a venue across time
observedvenuename Name for the specific venue
category The type of venue(bar, gym, bookstores, etc)
codedescriptorlist Notation used in Damron Guides for the venue, if applicable
placetype Whether the venue can be represented as a data type other than a point (polygon, lines)
address Street address, intersection, or major institution name (e.g., Shopping Mall, School, Park, etc.)
placenotes Additional information about the menu
locality Secondary geographical level, can be city, county or area
city the city where the venue is located
state The state where the venue is located
zip the zip code where the venue is located, if applicable
confidence The confidence level of the geocoder verifying the address
formaladdress Geocoded full address
ggl_measurementtype The type of measurement Google used to find the geocoded address
latitude The latitude of the venue's physical address, computed using geocoder
longitude The longitude of the venue's physical address, computed using geocoder
year The year this entry belongs to
comments Additional comments from the research team, if applicable
source The source of the information
createdby The identifier of the researcher who completed the data entry
geocoder The geocoder used to verify the location (Google/Mapbox)
dateadded The date when this entry was added to the table
lastmodified The date when this entry was last modified
ggl_placetype The place type of the current day location, provided by google
descriptorlist Any descriptors used by the platform to identify and classify venues
newcodelist Any new descriptors that were added since the data was originally added to the table
