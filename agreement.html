Shifting LGBTQ+ Spaces: Our User Agreement

We welcome and encourage contributions to our dataset! To protect against potentially malicious attacks on our platform and dataset, we require that you agree to not intentionally submit incorrect or false data. Once you agree to these conditions, you may start making contributions to the portal. Note that this process will NOT create a profile for you. We value your privacy and we do not collect any personal information, the passphrase and your contributions will not in any way be associated with your identity. Please also keep in mind that pass phrases will change occasionally for security reasons, so you may need to repeat this process if you wish to make additional contributions to the portal in the future.

(Optional) We would also like to hear what brings you to our platform, your motivations for contributing your knowledge, and your stories about how you came to be familiar with the local community. If your're comfortable sharing any of these, or if there's anything you may want us to know, please let us know in the text field below. Note that this process will NOT affect gaining access to a passphrase.
